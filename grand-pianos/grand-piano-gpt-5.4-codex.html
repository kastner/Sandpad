Keyboard

Drag across the keys for glissando. The keyboard opens centered around middle C.

This is still pure Web Audio synthesis, not sampled playback. The tone is shaped to feel like a close-mic grand rather than an electric piano: faster high-partial decay, longer bass bloom, slight unison beating, and a restrained soundboard-style reverb tail.
